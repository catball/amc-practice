2024 #4 Yunji added integers 1 to 9 but left one out. Her sum was a perfect square. Which number did she leave out?
  • (A) 5   (B) 6   (C) 7   (D) 8   (E) 9
Correct Answer: (E) 9
Solution: Sum of 1-9 is 45. 45 - 9 = 36 (a perfect square).
